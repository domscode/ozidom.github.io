How to play the game
You need to descend to the 3rd level of the dungeon. Each level is protected by a Staircase that can only be descended by solving a riddle. The riddle is a word that is either hidden in the text of ths song/riddle or is a word in song. Hints are available via a bolded character in the song. It gives you an idea where the word begins or the search for the word. The following is an example of how to dipher the song and the riddle...

Song
====
Waiting for the inevitable
Standing in the line
Longing for the ride
but it's always feeling
that makes you hide

Riddle
======
A the end of the line you will see me, slithering south

Solution
========
look at the bolded text that starts at the end of the third line. You will not see the yellow text in the game it's just a good way to see how the hidden words appear:

Waiting for the inevitable
Standing in the line
Longing for the ride
but it's always feeling
that makes you hide

Answer
======
eel
Other game rules
================
  • You only have 3 guesses - a staircase will explode after 3 guesses and the game is over
  • Monsters will try to capture you, the game is over when one of them reaches you
  • You can collect gold by picking it up - the perfect game is where the player has picked up all gold in the dungeon and answered solved four riddles

Instructions
  • {Arrow keys}move
  • [Enter] action - attempt to descend stairs
